Connect Google Sheets
One-time setup. Your Sheet becomes a live, readable database for all tender data.
1
Go to sheets.google.com and create a blank spreadsheet named CN Tender Tracker.
2
Click Extensions > Apps Script. Delete all code. Open google-apps-script-REV5.js, copy everything, paste it in. Save.
3
Click Deploy > New Deployment > Web app. Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone. Deploy and authorise.
4
Copy the Web App URL and paste it below.
5
After connecting, open the Users tab in your Sheet and add each team member: email, name, role, and TRUE/FALSE for admin.
