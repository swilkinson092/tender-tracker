SharePoint Lists Required
The following Lists must exist on your SharePoint site. They are created automatically the first time you use the app if they do not exist — but you need Owner or Full Control permissions for auto-creation to work.

Lists: TenderTracker_Users • TenderTracker_Tenders • TenderTracker_Progress • TenderTracker_Notes • TenderTracker_Assignments

To create Lists manually if auto-creation fails: go to your SharePoint site, click New > List, and create each one with the name above.
